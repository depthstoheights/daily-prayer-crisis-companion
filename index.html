Morning Prayer

Good Morning! 🌅
"Lord, as I begin this new day, I thank You for the gift of life and breath. Guide my steps, guard my heart, and help me to be a light to others. Grant me wisdom in my decisions, strength for my challenges, and peace in my spirit. May Your presence go before me and Your love surround me throughout this day. Amen."
✅ Morning prayer completed! Your day is blessed.

Evening Prayer

Good Evening! 🌙
"Heavenly Father, as this day comes to an end, I come before You with a grateful heart. Thank You for Your faithfulness throughout today. Forgive me where I have fallen short, and help me to rest in Your grace. Watch over my loved ones through the night, and prepare my heart for tomorrow's opportunities to serve You. Grant me peaceful sleep and sweet dreams. Amen."
✅ Evening prayer completed! Rest well in His peace.

Daily Prayer Prompt

Today's Focus
Gratitude & Trust

Today, focus your prayers on gratitude for God's provision and trust in His plan for your life. Reflect on three specific things you're grateful for and surrender any worries to His care.

"Give thanks in all circumstances; for this is God's will for you in Christ Jesus." - 1 Thessalonians 5:18
✅ Daily prompt completed! Your heart is aligned with His.

Emergency Prayer

God is with you right now 💙
"Lord, I come to You in this moment of need. You know my heart and my struggles. Please fill me with Your peace that surpasses understanding. Give me strength to face this challenge and remind me that You are always with me. Help me to trust in Your perfect timing and plan. Wrap me in Your love and comfort. I am safe in Your hands. Amen."
✅ You are loved and never alone. God's peace is with you.

📱 App Information

What this app does:
Daily Prayer + Crisis Support Companion provides daily prayer prompts, emergency scriptures, and spiritual support for your faith journey.
How to use:
• Start each day with Morning Prayer
• Get personalized daily prayer prompts
• Access emergency prayers and crisis support
• End your day with Evening Prayer
• Explore scripture library and resources
Install on your phone:
📱 iPhone: Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
📱 Android: Tap Menu → Add to Home Screen
This app works offline and saves your prayer progress locally on your device.
